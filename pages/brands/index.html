Jacobs Media Group brands

TRAVOLUTION

Travolution is the UK’s leading media brand for the digital travel industry, focusing on digital strategy and innovation. Launched in 2006, it comprises daily news on Travolution.co.uk and via a twice-weekly email alert; the Travolution Awards; the Travolution Summit, and a range of smaller events and partnerships.
